A Little About Me


I am a multidisciplinary artist who creates whimsical and vibrant things depending on whatever materials inspire me at the moment! I am a San Francisco native, so my art is greatly influenced by the colors, architecture, landscapes, local art and energies around me.
Growing up, i have always loved learning and using new mediums, crafts and techniques. my goal for 2021 is to add stained glass and tattooing to my repertoire!

My available art pieces are posted on Instagram @studio.aud. Contact me at hello.studioaud@gmail.com or send me a direct message on Instagram for inquiries, custom work or to purchase. I offer local pick up in San Francisco.
